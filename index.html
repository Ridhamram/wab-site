About Us

Thane is all set to witness a flurry of infrastructure come to life, with the administration sanctioning multiple ventures. These infrastructural projects will transform Thane into an urban city centre with excellent connectivity to Mumbai and other regions and this in turn will improve the city’s economic growth. Some of the initiatives are as follows:

  • Upcoming metro rail corridor connecting Thane-Ghatkopar-Wadala.

  • An excess of Rs. 500 cr. has been earmarked for development and upgradation of the road infrastructure across Thane city.

  • The state government is planning a 4 km long Coastal Road connecting Thane and Bhayander in order to ease the traffic on Ghodbunder Road.

  • The Twin Tube, a 3 lane Expressway connecting Thane and Borivali is also in the pipeline.
